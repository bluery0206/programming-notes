
BREAKPOINTS
    Breakpoints are widths that determine how your responsive 
        layout behaves across device or viewport sizes.

    Breakpoint	    Class infix	Dimensions
    Extra small	    None	    < 576px
    Small	        sm	        ≥ 576px
    Medium	        md	        ≥ 768px
    Large	        lg	        ≥ 992px
    Extra large	    xl	        ≥ 1200px
    Extra extra     large	    xxl	≥ 1400px
