
ACCESSIBILITY
    These classes below are for accessibility.
    The `visually-hidden` is for listeners, they will be not 
        be visible to people who can read but they read by 
        the reader if people chose to "read-out-loud" the page.

    <!-- for listeners only -->
    <p class="text-danger">
        <span class="visually-hidden">Danger: </span>
        This action is not reversible
    </p>

    <!-- for focusable components like buttons -->
    <a class="visually-hidden-focusable" href="#content">
        Skip to main content
    </a>
